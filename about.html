I'm interested in material that displays a sense of place, or that mixes genres, or that takes North American culture and re-interprets it. So I plan on talking "Mad Max" and "Coup de Torchon" alongside "The Searchers" and "Red River." I might also cover an obscure Yugloslav road movie, or some random Italian skin flick, because there's no sense in having a movie blog if you can't post about good movies. My name is Tom Bomba.
